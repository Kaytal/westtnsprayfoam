The How and Why!

Everyone knows that when your space isn’t properly insulated you are heating/cooling in excess.  Therefore you are literally throwing money out the window or, more metaphorically, you are throwing money out through the gaps in your insulation.

The common way to insulate large spaces up to now has always been to buy rolls of fiberglass/wool insulation batts.  These are then placed in between studs and you drywall around them. This doesn’t create an airtight seal.  Therefore you are losing the heat/cooling YOU paid for.

Spray foam applications offer a much more added value.  Modern consumers are looking for better, more energy efficient ways of heating/cooling.  The best way to do this is to not waste the energy they are already expending to heat/cool.  Spray foam is inexpensive, quick to be applied, and lasts much longer than traditional insulation.
